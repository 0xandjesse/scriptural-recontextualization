## Study Recommendations

### Starting Point
**Begin with The Gospel of Truth** - This text appears in both Codex I and Codex XII, making it excellent for comparative analysis and understanding textual variations. It's also foundational to Gnostic theology.

### Cross-Reference Opportunities
- Compare Gospel of Truth between Codex I and Codex XII when ready for Phase III
- Connect Apocryphon of James revelation themes to Gospel of Philip insights
- Note prayer/prayer tradition connections across Codex I texts
- Compare resurrection perspectives between Treatise and other texts
